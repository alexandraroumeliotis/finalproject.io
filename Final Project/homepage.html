Projects

Optic Eye

Optic Eye is amobile application and 3D printed lens to diagnose rare neurological disorders through using computer vision to detect optic disc swelling. I programmed this application after my grandfather was diagnsoed with a brain aneurysm that caused him to lose his vision. Frustrated by the lack of access to health care in low income immigrant communities, I wanted to find a way that people like my grandfather would have access to at-home healthcare and diagnostics.

The Coding League

The Coding League is an international youth-run nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting tech diversity by providing equal access to computer science education for female, low-income and other minority students. We are community-driven; we work to support local computer science non-profit organizations through technological resources, and foster a love of coding within local youth through engaging workshops and events.
